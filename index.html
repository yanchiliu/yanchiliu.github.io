I am a Researcher in the Department of Data Science and System Security at the NEC Labs America. I got my Ph.D. in Information Technology from Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Professor Hui Xiong. My research focuses on data science, artificial intelligence, and business analytics. Here you can find my LinkedIn Page, and my Google Scholar (GS citations~2000, h-index=21).

I have a broad interest in data science, artificial intelligence, and business analytics, with a particular interest in smart cities and mobile recommender systems. My current research develops efficient and effective techniques to address various challenges in business and city development, to discover and understand the relationship among residents, businesses, and urban environment, and to ultimately assist intelligent decision-making. My interests also extend to transportation, social networks, and clustering validation.
